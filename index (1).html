Dear Mary,

If you are reading this, it means you opened it — and that already makes me love you more.

There are things I have wanted to say, things that do not come out right when I speak, so I wrote them instead. You deserve more than clumsy words. You deserve the careful ones.

I am glad you exist. I am glad you are mine. I hope every day I get to say that again.

Yours, always.
